# HikkInn — Market Research: North Pakistan Hospitality & Travel

**Prepared:** July 2026 · **Audience:** Investors, project sponsors, steering committee
**Basis:** Live web research (Jul 2026) — see `docs/research/` for raw sources. Items not
verifiable on public pages are marked **[NV]** (no verification) and flagged for primary-data acquisition.

---

## 1. Executive summary

Northern Pakistan (Gilgit-Baltistan + Khyber Pakhtunkhwa highlands) is one of the
fastest-growing adventure/tourism regions in South Asia, yet its hospitality and
travel sector still runs largely on **manual, phone/agent/walk-in systems**. There
is no centralized, digital, two-sided platform covering **stays + meals + live
transport + charter activities**. HikkInn fills that gap.

The supply exists and is sizable (e.g. Airbnb alone lists **529 stays in Skardu,
475 in Hunza, 121 in Naran, 86 in Swat** as of Jul 2026), but it is fragmented
across OTAs, operator websites, WhatsApp, and walk-in — and the long-tail
(budget guest houses, jeeps, adventure add-ons) is largely **undigitized**.

## 3. Price architecture (PKR/night, verified ranges)

- **Budget:** Rs. 150–1,000 (older Wikivoyage data; mostly phone-only).
- **Standard:** Rs. 3,000–8,000 (e.g. Naran River Whisper double Rs. 4,500;
  Swat PTDC single/double Rs. 3,200/5,500).
- **Premium/Resort:** Rs. 8,000+ or US$200+ (Shangrila, Serena Shigar Fort
  Residence "from Rs. 8,000", Serena Swat US$225 avg).

## 4. Air connectivity (verified)

- **Skardu Int'l (KDU):** ~8–10 flights/day; PIA from Islamabad, Lahore,
  Multan, Faisalabad, Sialkot, Karachi. Weather-dependent (mountain VFR).
- **Gilgit (GIL):** PIA Islamabad only (42-seat; "often delayed by several days
  in winter"); ATR-42/C-130 only.
- **AirSial:** lists Skardu sector (likely seasonal/charter).
- **Swat (Saidu Sharif):** PIA 3× weekly from Islamabad (~30 min).
- "Sehrai Air" **[NV]** — likely confusion with Serene Air / AirSial / K2 Airways.

## 5. Road & ground (verified)

- KKH: Hunza ~100 km / 2–3 hrs from Gilgit; Islamabad→Hunza up to 24 hrs.
- Skardu road: Islamabad→Skardu 16–20 hrs; Gilgit→Skardu <200 km but ~8 hrs;
  landslide-prone.
- Naran: gateway to Gilgit-Hunza via Babusar Pass (summer).
- Shared jeeps common (Naran→Saiful Muluk Rs. 1,500). ATV/quad rental **[NV]**.

## 8. Market pain points (evidence-based)

1. Weather-dependent flights, multi-day cancellations, no live rebooking.
2. Phone/agent/walk-in dominant for budget stock — no live availability/payment.
3. No centralized digital inventory for North Pakistan (stays + meals + transport
   + activities fragmented).
4. Weak OTA penetration for traditional hotels.
5. Discovery/trust gap for adventure products (paragliding/heli/ATV unverified).
6. Seasonality & road fragility with manual rerouting.
7. Carrier ambiguity (real vs unverified names).

## 10. So what? (investment thesis)

A fragmented, growing, underserved market with real supply but no digital
aggregation layer. First mover that digitizes the **long-tail + live services**
(meals, transport, charter) captures the consolidation premium. HikkInn's wedge:
**one session, one app — stay + eat + move + fly.**
