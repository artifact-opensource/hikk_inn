# Research: Current Hospitality & Travel Services — North Pakistan (Jul 2026)

> Subagent research deliverable. Search engines bot-blocked → direct-to-source.
> **[NO DATA FOUND]** = could not verify on a readable page (never fabricated).

## 1. Inventory & booking mode
- **Online-capable**: Airbnb (Skardu 529 / Hunza 475 / Naran 121 / Swat 86
  paid stays), Bookme.pk (Hotel vertical), Sastaticket.pk, international OTAs.
- **Manual/phone/walk-in dominant** for local guest houses & jeep services —
  Wikivoyage lists properties by phone number; many have no URL/email.
- Implication: large share of inventory (budget stays, jeep, adventure add-ons)
  is **not digitally bookable**.

## 2. Price ranges (PKR/night)
- Budget: Rs. 150–1,000 (older data). Standard: Rs. 3,000–8,000. Premium:
  Rs. 8,000+ / US$200+ (Shangrila, Serena Shigar, Serena Swat).

## 5. Adventure operators
- Trekking/mountaineering: Adventure Tours Pakistan, Hunza Guides.
- Skiing, gliding, white-water, air-safari listed; **paragliding / heli / ATV

## 6. Pain points (manual systems)
Weather cancellations w/o rebooking · phone/agent booking · no centralized
digital inventory · weak OTA penetration · adventure discovery/trust gap ·
seasonal road fragility · carrier ambiguity.
